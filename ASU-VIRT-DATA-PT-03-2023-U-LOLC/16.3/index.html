Data Ethics Week

This is not the most exciting piece of knowledge you may want to know. However, all universities are duty-bound to teach data ethics.

And it is not as trivial as it seems. Data governance is a very important subject for all major corporations. We need to be able to give an account on:

  • What data we are collecting?
  • How are we using the data?

Because a lawsuit could mean millions of dollars. Often, a outsourced auditing company may go through the data processes within the organization you're working for, and you will need to be able to account to them as needed.

Also, data governance jobs is a six-figure job: https://www.talent.com/salary?job=data+governance

Data Regulation

We don't have to know every regulation under the sun to be effective. Typically, we focus on the regulations that affect our industry where we are working in.

I'm familiar with General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), but not the rest since I don't work much in the other sectors.
